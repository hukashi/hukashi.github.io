KIDPANION

Kidpanion was created to be a resource for parents with small children. Parents will have access to user created data to find out if a point of interest has the necessary amenities to make a family outing as enjoyable as possible. The web application will also give users ratings and feedback from other users. It will take the guess work out for parents who are looking to have a pleasant time with their little ones.

TRACK THE HUNT

This project is a web application that will help users track the progress of their job hunt. This will take all the information necessary such as company information as well as the contact information for the company. It will allow for updating the progress with that company such as follow-ups, status, completion etc. This will is a tool that should help anyone looking for a job to help stay organized and maintain contact with the companies at all times with the most up to date information of the status of how far into the process they are in.
